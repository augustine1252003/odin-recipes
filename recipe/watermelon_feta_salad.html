Watermelon Feta Salad

This chunky watermelon feta salad with arugula and tomatoes is enjoyed by all. Thanks to the watermelon, the kids also like it.

Nutriton Facts

  • Calories: 131
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbs: 12g
  • Protein: 3g

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons Of Olive Oil
  • 2 Teaspoons Of White Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 8 Ounces Grape Tomatoes, Halved
  • 3 Cups Of Arugula
  • 1 Cup Sliced Red Onion
  • 2 Pounds Watermelon, Cubed
  • 4 Pounds Feta Cheese, Cut Into 1/4 Cubes

Directions

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar and salt together in a large mixing bowl. Add tomatoes, arugula and red onion; toss to coat. Gently stir in watermelon and feta cheese to serve.
